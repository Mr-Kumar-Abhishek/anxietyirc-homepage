Connecting to the #anxiety support channel

To connect you will need an IRC chat client:

Application clients for Mac

Adium, Colloquy , Limechat

Application clients for Windows

mIRC, Hexchat

Application clients for iOS / Android

Limechat for iOS, Colloquy for iOS, AndroIRC - Android , Andchat - Android


And use the following settings to connect:

Host/Server: us.undernet.org or eu.undernet.org

Room: #anxiety
